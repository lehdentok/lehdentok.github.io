Leh den tok

… a place to talk and learn about what you can do with technology—in your language.

What do you want to do with technology?

  • Building a community of young Sierra Leoneans interested in technology and the Internet, enabling them to connect with each other to make exciting things happen.
  • Bringing together resources to help you learn about what you can do with technology.
  • Organising classes and seminars so that you can meet other young people interested in technology face-to-face and learn from experts and your peers.
  • Bringing affordable computing to Sierra Leone by importing laptops and other devices at a low price.
